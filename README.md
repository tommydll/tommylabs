# About

TommyLabs is my personal platform and long-term digital space.

The goal is not only to present my résumé or projects, but also to progressively build an immersive platform that reflects:
- my engineering journey
- my technical growth
- my projects and experiments
- my way of thinking about software
- my design and infrastructure interests

This platform will evolve over time into a mix of:
- portfolio
- engineering journal
- technical playground
- digital garden
- experimentation lab

# Core Objectives

- Showcase my professional experience and technical skills
- Present projects with detailed technical breakdowns
